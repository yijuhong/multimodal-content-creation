# Setup

## Brand Decision

- Brand name: LumaRest
- Brand type: Virtual AI wellness app
- Target audience: 20s-30s office workers who need support for stress, sleep, and focus management
- Tone and manner: Calm, soft light, quiet recovery, minimal premium mood
- USP: AI recommends breathing, meditation, and sleep sound routines based on the user's condition and time of day
- Campaign goal: Build calm brand awareness
- Core message: "일상의 소음을 낮추고, 나에게 맞는 회복 루틴을 켜다."

## File Management Rules

- Scene numbering: Use two-digit scene IDs such as `scene01` and `scene02`.
- Filename pattern: `{scene}_{asset-type}_{purpose-or-tool}_v{version}.{ext}`
- Prompt log pattern: `{scene}_promptlog_v{version}.md`
- Final storyboard filename pattern: `LumaRest_storyboard_v01.pdf`
- Final video filename pattern: `LumaRest_brand_ad_v01.mp4`
